  • description: Class that evaluates the spectrum for a DM particle of a given mass, channel, cross section, and J-factor. Combines Pythia-based tables from both Fermi (2 GeV < m_DM < 10 TeV) and HAWC (10 TeV < m_dm < 1 PeV) The parameterization is given by F(x) = 1 / (8 * pi) * (1/mass^2) * sigmav * J * dN/dE(E,mass,i) Note that this class assumes that mass and J-factor are provided in units of GeV and GeV^2 cm^-5
